**Project Title:**
CyberShield: Interactive Cyber Warfare & Cryptography Visualization Platform

**Project Description:**
CyberShield is a web-based cybersecurity simulation platform designed to demonstrate real-world cyber attacks and their corresponding defense mechanisms through interactive visualizations. The system enables users to simulate attacks such as Man-in-the-Middle (MITM), Packet Sniffing, Replay Attacks, and Brute Force Attacks while applying security techniques including AES encryption, RSA encryption, and Digital Signatures.

The platform provides a dual-perspective environment where users can observe both attacker and defender views in real time, helping them understand how vulnerabilities are exploited and how cryptographic solutions protect communication systems. Through animated packet flows, live attack simulations, and security monitoring dashboards, CyberShield transforms complex Cryptography and Network Security concepts into an engaging and practical learning experience.
